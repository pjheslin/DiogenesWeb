DiogenesWeb

DiogenesWeb is an web application for reading XML files of Latin and Ancient Greek on handheld devices. It was written by Peter Heslin and it is a project of the Department of Classics and Ancient History, Durham University.

Feedback

Send feedback — expressions of thanks, bug reports and feaature requests — to p.j.heslin@durham.ac.uk. We are particularly keen to hear about how this applciation is used in the classroom and by non-academic users.

Credits

DiogenesWeb includes a great deal of open-access material — texts, morphological data and lexica — produced by the Perseus project. It also includes contributions from Logeion. It can read the XML files exported by the Diogenes desktop application from the Packard Humanities Institute and Thesaurus Linguae Graecae CD-ROM databases, if you upload them to your private cloud storage.
